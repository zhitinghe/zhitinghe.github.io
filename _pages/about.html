About Me

Hi! I'm a recent M.A. graduate in Digital Media Art from Communication University of China. I’m passionate about how emerging technologies can empower individuals and enhance well-being. Therefore, my research has focused on using VR to design immersive experiences that evoke awe, a profound and positive emotion, and leveraging AI to help individuals engage with local cultural narratives.

Research

“I Feel Myself So Small!”: Designing and Evaluating VR Awe Experiences Based on Theories Related to the Sublime

Authors: He, Z., Fan, M., Guo, X., Zhao, Y., Wang, Y. (2024)

Abstract: This study explores the design and evaluation of VR experiences aimed at evoking the emotion of awe, based on theories of the sublime. We review existing theories, propose new design strategies, and test them through a series of experiments.

Methodology: The research involved the design and development of VR prototypes, conducting user studies, and performing both quantitative and qualitative analysis to assess the impact of these experiences.
