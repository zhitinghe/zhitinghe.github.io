CSCW’25

Broadly Applicable Awe-Inspiring Design Elements

This research project aims to develop design principles for creating various types of VR awe experiences that users can switch between to maintain the quality of awe. It also seeks to understand the role of VR in inducing awe compared to video and whether the effectiveness of media varies across different types of awe experiences.
