Broadly Applicable Awe-Inspiring Design Elements

A multi-scene design exploration translating awe-related design elements into five VR scene concepts, with the broader goal of understanding how different awe structures may function across VR and video media.

Project Overview

This project explored how different awe-inducing design elements could be translated into immersive scene concepts. Instead of focusing on a single awe experience, it developed five distinct VR scene directions, each emphasizing a different type of awe trigger, such as vast scale, complex structure, abstract concepts, extraordinary power, or high-value symbolic objects.

The work was intended as a concept design exploration rather than a single final prototype, with the goal of examining how different awe structures could be designed, compared, and later adapted into production.

Initial Scene Planning

This board documents the initial concept exploration for the five awe scenes. Some of these ideas were later refined or adapted during development based on time, feasibility, and production constraints.

Design Framework

The five scene concepts were developed from a broader mapping of awe-related design factors, including vast elements, complex structure, abstract concepts, extraordinary power or spirit, and high-value symbolic objects.

Five Scene Concepts

The table below summarizes the five initial scene concepts, the intended awe object in each case, and the design facets explored through each direction.

Scene 1 — Mountain Views

A natural landscape concept centered on vast size and horizon, emphasizing openness, distance, and environmental scale.

Scene 2 — Water Freezing

A scene built around complex structure, using the process of water freezing to reveal unfamiliar patterns and hidden order.

Scene 3 — Light Speed

A concept exploring awe through abstract scale and great concepts, translating the idea of extreme speed into an experiential journey.

Scene 4 — Indoor Fires

A scene based on extraordinary power and threat, focusing on the rapid spread of fire and the user’s perception of overwhelming force.

Scene 5 — Cultural Relic

A symbolic scene centered on high-value cultural objects, designed to evoke awe through historical significance, meaning, and reverence.
